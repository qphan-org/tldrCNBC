About

TL;DR CNBC scrapes and analyzes articles published on CNBC

For each article, there will be:

  • a summary with less than 150 words so that the reading time is less than 30 seconds, helping readers get the main ideas of the articles in the fastest way possible.
  • a sentiment descryption (POSITVIE/NEGATIVE/NEUTRAL/MIXED)
  • and a list of keywords mentioned in the article
